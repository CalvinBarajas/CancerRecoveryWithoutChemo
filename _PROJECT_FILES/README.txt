CancerRecoveryWithoutChemo v.1.0 Release
------------------------------------

CancerRecoveryWithoutChemo is an informational medical app which helps those who are interested in exploring cancer information and inexpensive and effective natural alternative cancer cures and therapies.  Alternative therapies are becoming more mainstream every year as more scientifically-based studies are conducted.  We now have a multitude of options (from organic raw vegan to special dietary supplementation) which have become proven alternatives to chemotherapy.
